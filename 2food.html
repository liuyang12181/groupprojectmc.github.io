Canada: French fries with cheese and gravy

Potato chips with cheese and gravy, one of the unique snacks in Quebec, Canada.

Ingredients include chips, grainy cheese cubes and gravy, sometimes with ham, hot dog sausage or salmon.

Quick-witted McDonald's has added this special snack to its deluxe menu of fries topped with gravy and cheese chunks.
